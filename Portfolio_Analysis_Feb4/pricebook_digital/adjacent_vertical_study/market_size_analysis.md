## 1. Introduction

This document provides a market size analysis of the Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) software market and the electrical estimating software sub-market. This analysis is a key component of the Adjacent Vertical Feasibility Study for Pricebook Digital.

## 2. MEP Software Market

The global MEP software market is experiencing robust growth. The market was valued at **$4.0 billion in 2023** and is projected to reach **$9.6 billion by 2033**, expanding at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of **9.2%** [1]. This growth is driven by the increasing adoption of Building Information Modeling (BIM) and the need for more efficient and integrated solutions in the construction industry.

## 3. Electrical Estimating Software Market

The electrical estimating software market, a significant segment of the broader MEP market, is also demonstrating strong growth. This market was valued at **$2 billion in 2024** and is forecasted to grow to **$4.08 billion by 2031**, at a CAGR of **10.23%** [2]. The increasing complexity of electrical systems and the need for accurate and timely cost estimates are key drivers of this growth.
